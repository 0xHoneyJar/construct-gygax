# Lore

Surface TTRPG design wisdom -- heuristics, common patterns, known anti-patterns, and lessons from published game systems. This skill provides contextual design guidance grounded in decades of tabletop game design practice, helping designers avoid known pitfalls and leverage proven approaches.
